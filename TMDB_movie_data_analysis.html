Conclusions¶

In this project, I analyzed the TMDB movie data and compared the revenue with the budget, popularity, and vote count. I found out that movies with a higher budget, popularity, or vote count will bring much higher revenue. I think my finding is reasonable because movies with more budget will have better quality and promotion. The more popular the movie is, the more people will interest in watching it. It will also improve revenue.
